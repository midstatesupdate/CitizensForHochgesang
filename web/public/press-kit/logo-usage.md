# Campaign Logo Usage Guidelines

## Allowed
- Use the current official logo file exactly as provided.
- Preserve aspect ratio.
- Keep adequate clear space around the logo.
- Use high-contrast backgrounds.

## Not Allowed
- Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.
- Do not alter logo colors.
- Do not place logo on low-contrast or visually noisy backgrounds.

## Source of Truth
The latest official logo should be maintained in Sanity `siteSettings.campaignLogo` and mirrored in `/press` resources.
